All About Taylor Swift

Taylor Swift is 32 years old. She was born in West Reading, Pennsylvania on December 13, 1989. She is a singer and songwriter, and has written over 200 songs and 10 studio albums. Her most resent album is Midnights, which she released on October 21 at midnight. She is also in the process of re-recording her first 6 albums, in order to get her master for them. They were sold without her consent, and she had already released Fearless and Red (Taylor's Version). She has a honorary doctorate from NYU in Fine Arts. Her lucky number is 13, she has 3 cats and a boyfriend of 6 years, Joe Alwyn.

Some of her most popular songs are Shake it off, You Belong With Me, Blank Space, Don't Blame Me, Cardigan, All Too Well, and Picture to Burn. Taylor Swift's albums cover multiple genre's, she started out with country, moved to pop, and over quarantine, wrote 2 albums with an alternative/indie vibe. Her work has been ranked among the most succesful artist in the world, including The Beatles and Mariah Carey. Her songs range from different vocal ranges, song writing styles, and emotions.
